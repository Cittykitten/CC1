Effects of Climate Change in Ghana

Our Future, Our Fight:
The Real Effects of Climate Change in Ghana:

Irregular Rainfall

Rainfall comes at the wrong times or in the wrong amounts, making it hard for farmers to grow crops.

Read more

Flooding

Heavy rains flood streets and homes, damaging property and spreading disease.

Read more

Droughts

Drought dries up rivers, kills crops, and leaves people and animals without water.

Read more

Extreme Heat

Hotter temperatures make daily life uncomfortable and increase the risk of heat-related sickness.

Read more

Food Insecurity

Because of droughts and floods, farms produce less food, leading to hunger and higher food prices.

Read more

Displacement

People may have to move due to floods, sea level rise, or extreme weather.

Read more

Coastal Erosion

Rising sea levels erode Ghana's coast, damaging homes, roads, and farms.

Read more

Ocean acidification

Increased CO₂ lowers ocean pH, harming
marine life.
